Common Disorders in Women

PCOD (Polycystic Ovary Disorder)

PCOD impacts both physical and mental health, often leading to anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem.

Postpartum Depression

A condition affecting new mothers, characterized by feelings of sadness, anxiety, and exhaustion.

Menstrual Disorders

Conditions like PMS and PMDD affect emotional well-being and mental health during the menstrual cycle.

Mental Health Challenges at Different Life Stages

Adolescence

Adolescents face challenges like body image issues, social pressures, and hormonal changes.

Motherhood

Balancing family, work, and personal needs can lead to mental health struggles in mothers.

Middle Age and Senior Years

Women may experience mental health issues related to menopause, loneliness, or grief.

Resources and Coping Mechanisms

Here are some tips and resources to improve mental health:

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation.
  • Seek professional help when needed.
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle with a balanced diet and regular exercise.
  • Join support groups for women facing similar challenges.
