Your Daily Healthy Meal Plan

Breakfast

Start your day with a nutritious and filling breakfast to fuel your body.

Example: Oatmeal with Berries & Almond Butter

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/4 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Nutrition: 350 kcal, 8g protein, 15g fiber, 15g fat

Lunch

A light yet satisfying meal to keep you energized throughout the afternoon.

Example: Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Grilled chicken breast (4 oz)
  • Mixed greens (spinach, arugula, kale)
  • Cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado
  • Olive oil and balsamic vinegar dressing

Nutrition: 400 kcal, 30g protein, 10g carbs, 25g fat

Dinner

A hearty and healthy dinner to end your day on a nourishing note.

Example: Quinoa with Roasted Veggies & Salmon

  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • Roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots)
  • Salmon fillet (4 oz)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Nutrition: 500 kcal, 30g protein, 40g carbs, 25g fat
